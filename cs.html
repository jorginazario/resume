Why Computer Science?

I consider that a person's ability to create with nothing more than a computer is revolutionary. Computers have empowered people to create abstract worlds of information and tools without the need of any major investments. Computer Science as a major provides me with all the knowledge and skills I need to empower my mind to create what it desires. CSE challenges me to think abstractly about the organization of complex data, algorithms, and functions, and to learn about the "magic" that happens inside a computer. Understanding the "magic" of how billions of transistor components come together to form logic gates that give life to combinational logic is just fascinating to me. I feel priviliged and proud to call myself a computer scientist.
